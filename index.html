⚠ EMERGENCY PROCEDURES
Memorise these actions. In-flight response time is measured in seconds.
RC Link Loss — 915MHz (Ground → Carrier)
1
Short loss (≤1.5s): ArduPlane switches to FBWA automatically. Regain link and resume.
2
Long loss (>5s): RTL triggers at 120m AGL. Do NOT power-cycle TX in flight.
3
GCS (SIYI HM30) still available: send mode commands via Mission Planner.
Relay Link Loss — 2.4GHz (Carrier → FPV)
1
FPV failsafe activates. Co-pilot ceases inputs immediately.
2
Carrier Pilot holds loiter — sub-drone may be RTH-ing toward carrier position.
3
If relay does not recover: co-pilot monitors FPV failsafe. Carrier Pilot recovers independently.
Video Loss — 5.8GHz or SIYI Chain
1
Co-pilot: zero inputs. Sub-drone to hover/stabilise.
2
Carrier Pilot: descend carrier to reduce path loss. Wait 10s.
3
Not recovered: FPV RTH. Carrier Pilot recovers carrier independently.
Transition Failure — VTOL → FW
1
No airspeed build within 3s or excessive pitch-down: QHOVER immediately.
2
Q_TRANS_FAIL=4s — auto-abort triggers at 4s. Do not wait for it.
3
Land in VTOL. Inspect tilt servo, cruise ESC, battery voltage before next attempt.
Battery Critical — Carrier
1
No BATT_LOW_VOLT set. First automated action: 22.2V → Loiter to QLand at current position. Will NOT return home.
2
Initiate manual RTL at 24V. If dropping rapidly: Nomad left button → RTL immediately.
3
If Loiter→QLand triggers: confirm area below is clear. Aircraft descending vertically from current position.
GCS Link Loss (SIYI HM30)
1
Switch to physical ELRS 915MHz TX immediately. Confirm carrier responds.
2
In AUTO: GCS failsafe triggers RTL. In FBWB/QHOVER: no failsafe — fly manually.
3
Use Nomad 3-position switch (QHOVER/FBWB/AUTO) or left button for RTL.

Ground Station Teardown
1
Check mast battery voltage

Disconnect and measure 6S 3000mAh mast battery. Below 21.0V: charge immediately, do not store discharged.

2

3

4

4
