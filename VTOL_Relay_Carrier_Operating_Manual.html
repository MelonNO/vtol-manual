BATT_LOW_VOLT is not set. There is no low-battery warning before the critical failsafe at 22.2V. A 6S pack at 22.2V is at nominal — this is very late to be triggering. Recommend setting BATT_LOW_VOLT to ~23.1V (3.85V/cell) to get a RTL warning with flight time remaining. Initiating manual return at ~24V (4.0V/cell) is advised.
Tilt-Rotor Configuration

T2 uses front-pair tilting rotors (Q_TILT_MASK=3, Q_TILT_TYPE=2, max 80°). Front motors provide both VTOL lift and forward thrust. Rear pair provides VTOL lift only. Transition time Q_TRANSITION_MS=3000ms; fail-timeout Q_TRANS_FAIL=4s. Back-transition: Q_BACKTRANS_MS=10000ms.

VTOL assist activates below 9 m/s airspeed or below 25m AGL in fixed-wing modes. Hover thrust learned: Q_M_THST_HOVER=0.272 (~27% throttle).

Power-Up Sequence
Power up in this order every time. Reversing sequence risks arming motors unexpectedly on link establishment.
Sequence
1
SIYI HM30 ground unit

Power mast unit (6S 3000mAh). Confirm HM30 link LED. Open MPV.net — no video expected yet.

2
FPV sub-drone

Power FPV. Confirm Betaflight boots, ELRS RX 2.4GHz shows connected. VTX begins transmitting 5.8GHz.

3
Carrier drone

Connect single 6S pack — all carrier systems power on together (FC, DBR4 relay TX, 5.8G VRX, CVBS→HDMI, SIYI chain). Wait for ArduPlane boot tone. DBR4 establishes ELRS 2.4GHz link to FPV. SIYI air unit links to HM30. 5.8G VRX acquires FPV signal → video appears in MPV.net.

4
ELRS 915MHz TX (last)

Power physical RC controller. ELRS 915MHz link to carrier establishes. Confirm in Mission Planner — RC inputs visible, no pre-arm errors.

Takeoff — VTOL Phase
1
Mode: QHOVER

Confirm switch position 1. Confirm mode in Mission Planner HUD.

2
Arm

Stick arm or GCS arm. All 4 motors spool to Q_M_SPIN_ARM. Confirm no pre-arm messages.

3
Hover check at 2–3m

Confirm stable hover, no oscillation (roll P=0.25, pitch P=0.25 — oscillation at low alt = land immediately). Confirm SIYI link stable, ELRS 868 RSSI acceptable. Co-pilot confirms telemetry nominal in MP.

4
Climb to transition altitude

Climb in QHOVER to ≥25m AGL before transitioning. Wind vane active (Q_WVANE_ENABLE=1) — aircraft will weathervane into wind automatically.

Transition — VTOL → Fixed Wing
Transition is the highest-risk phase. Front tilt motors rotate forward. Altitude loss is normal but must not exceed ~5m. If aircraft pitches down aggressively or airspeed does not build within 4s (Q_TRANS_FAIL=4s): revert to QHOVER immediately.
1
Switch to FBWB

switch position 2. Front tilt motors begin rotating forward. Cruise motor spools.

2
Monitor 3s transition window

Target airspeed 13 m/s (cruise). Min airspeed 11 m/s. If no acceleration: QHOVER immediately.

3
Transition confirmed

VTOL motors stop. Tilt locked forward. Aircraft in fixed-wing flight. Co-pilot advances mission to WP2 in Mission Planner if in AUTO.

4
Switch to AUTO

switch position 3. Carrier enters mission. Carrier Pilot takes over MP monitoring. Co-pilot clears to operate FPV sub-drone.

Relay Operations
1
Loiter established — FPV handover

Carrier in AUTO mission loiter. Carrier Carrier Pilot confirms relay RSSI to FPV and video stream in MPV.net. Verbal confirmation between pilots. Co-pilot takes FPV TX and begins operating sub-drone.

2
Carrier Carrier Pilot: orbit management

Primary duty is keeping the FPV inside the relay bubble. Monitor FPV position relative to carrier via video feed and verbal calls from FPV pilot. Adjust carrier orbit using GUIDED mode or waypoint edits so the carrier orbits around the FPV's operating area with FPV kept on the near (close) side of the orbit. If FPV approaches relay range edge: call "RELAY EDGE" and vector carrier to close distance before switching to GUIDED.

3
Carrier Carrier Pilot: continuous monitoring

Watch carrier battery voltage (no low-voltage warning — manual only), carrier altitude, SIYI HM30 link quality, and SIYI IP camera for situational awareness. Carrier Pilot may switch to GUIDED, FBWB, or any mode at any time without consultation.

4
Battery discipline

No BATT_LOW_VOLT configured — voltage monitoring is manual. Target RTL initiation at 24.0V (~4.0V/cell). Hard limit: RTL before 22.8V. At 22.2V: automated Loiter→QLand at current position.

FPV pilot verbal callouts are the carrier Carrier Pilot's only source of FPV status. Establish a call cadence: battery level, position, and any issues every 2–3 minutes minimum.
Recovery
1
Recall FPV

Co-pilot returns FPV sub-drone first where possible. Confirm landed or safe loiter.

2
Carrier return

Mission WP5 (LOITER_TO_ALT down) → WP6 (slope-in) → WP7 (VTOL_LAND). Or manual RTL via CH5 switch.

3
Back-transition

10s back-transition window (Q_BACKTRANS_MS=10000). Front motors tilt back to vertical. Monitor for smooth transition — if oscillation appears: switch QHOVER manually.

4
QLAND / QHOVER land

Final descent. Confirm landing zone clear. Disarm on touchdown. Q_LAND_FINAL_ALT=1.5m → final descent at Q_LAND_FINAL_SPD=0.2 m/s.

Memorise these actions. In-flight response time is measured in seconds.
RC Link Loss — 868MHz (Ground → Carrier)
1
Short loss (≤1.5s): ArduPlane switches to FBWA automatically. Regain link and resume.
2
Long loss (>5s): ArduPlane triggers RTL at 120m AGL. Do NOT power-cycle TX during flight — reconnect on live link can cause RC spike.
3
GCS (SIYI HM30) still available: send mode commands via Mission Planner. GCS failsafe only active in AUTO mode.
Relay Link Loss — 2.4GHz (Carrier → FPV)
1
FPV Betaflight failsafe activates. Co-pilot ceases inputs immediately.
2
Carrier Pilot holds carrier loiter position — do not reposition carrier aggressively, sub-drone may be RTH-ing toward last known carrier position.
3
If relay does not recover: co-pilot monitors FPV failsafe landing. Carrier Pilot initiates carrier recovery independently.
Video Loss — 5.8GHz or SIYI Chain
1
Co-pilot: zero inputs on FPV TX. Sub-drone to hover/stabilise.
2
Carrier Pilot: descend carrier altitude to reduce path loss. Wait 10s.
3
Not recovered: co-pilot activates FPV sub-drone RTH. Carrier Pilot recovers carrier independently.
4
Check: 5.8G VRX channel match, CVBS→HDMI converter power, SIYI air unit link to HM30.
Transition Failure — VTOL → FW
1
If airspeed not building within 3s or pitch-down excessive: QHOVER immediately.
2
Q_TRANS_FAIL=4s — ArduPlane will abort transition automatically at 4s if airspeed threshold not met. Do not wait.
3
Return to land in VTOL. Inspect: tilt servo travel, cruise motor ESC, Q_TILT_MAX, battery voltage.
Battery Critical — Carrier
1
No BATT_LOW_VOLT set — first automated action is BATT_CRT_VOLT=22.2V → BATT_FS_CRT_ACT=6 (Loiter to QLand). Aircraft will loiter to bleed airspeed then descend vertically. It will NOT return home — it lands where it is.
2
Do not allow voltage to reach 22.2V in flight. Return manually at 24V. If voltage drops rapidly: immediate RTL via Nomad left button before critical threshold.
3
If Loiter→QLand triggers unexpectedly: confirm landing zone below is clear. Aircraft is committed to descending vertically from current position.
GCS Link Loss (SIYI HM30)
1
Switch to physical ELRS 915MHz TX immediately. Confirm carrier responds to RC inputs.
2
In AUTO: FS_GCS_ENABL=2 triggers RTL only when in AUTO. In FBWB/QHOVER: GCS loss does not trigger failsafe — fly manually.
3
Note: no Mission Planner available for mode changes via GCS — use Nomad 3-position switch (QHOVER / FBWB / AUTO) or left button for RTL.
Immediate Post-Landing
1
Disarm carrier

Confirm disarmed in Mission Planner. All motors stopped. Do not approach until disarmed.

2
Disarm FPV sub-drone

Co-pilot confirms FPV disarmed. Props stopped.

3
Disconnect carrier battery

Disconnect 6S pack immediately after disarm. All carrier systems — FC, relay Nomad, SIYI chain, VRX — power down simultaneously. Record landing voltage.

4
Disconnect FPV battery

Record FPV landing voltage.

Ground Station Teardown
1
Check mast battery voltage

Disconnect and measure the 6S 3000mAh mast battery. Record voltage. If below 21.0V (3.5V/cell): charge immediately, do not store discharged. Label if marginal.

2
Power down SIYI HM30 ground unit

Power down HM30 on mast. Disconnect ethernet cable. Remove mast battery safely.

3
Lower and secure mast

Confirm HM30 and cabling secured before transport.

4
Close Mission Planner

Save log if required. Close MP and GStreamer/MPV.net.

Inspection
1
Carrier airframe

Inspect tilt motor mounts, wing joins, prop blades, and all connectors. Check for any heat or unusual smell from ESCs or motors.

2
Carrier battery

Check for puffing, heat, or damage. Store at storage voltage (22.2V / 3.7V/cell) if not flying within 24h.

3
Video chain

Check CVBS→HDMI converter and SIYI HDMI→IP for heat. Inspect VRX antenna condition.

4
FPV sub-drone

Inspect props, motors, and frame. Check for damage from any FPV flying.
